About me

I am a recently graduated engineering graduate in Process and Innovation from DTU. Through my studies and especially my time at Demant, I have specialised in UX — from user research and workshop facilitation to prototyping and communicating insights to decision-makers.

What drives me is the intersection of the technical and the human. My engineering background means I understand systems and can talk with developers and specialists — while always holding on to the fact that technology only creates value when it actually works for the people using it.

I have previously worked at LiqTech and Grundfos, and I am based in Aarhus. When I am not working, you will often find me playing floorball, out on a run, or deep in a knitting project.
