About me

I am a newly graduated BEng in Process and Innovation from DTU, specialising in UX research and human-centred product development. During my time at Demant, I worked across the research process — from participant recruitment and study design to interviews, analysis, prototyping and stakeholder communication.

I enjoy making complex products easier to understand and use. My engineering background helps me collaborate with developers and specialists, while my UX experience keeps users’ needs central throughout the process.

With experience from Demant, LiqTech and Grundfos, I bring a broad perspective on product development, research and collaboration. I am now based in Aarhus and curious about opportunities where technology, users and meaningful solutions meet.
